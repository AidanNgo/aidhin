WHAT'S UP

My name is Aidan Ngo, and I’m a photographer based in Austin, Texas. I’m currently a junior attending the University of Texas.

I seriously love taking pictures, particularly street photography and portraits; you will definitely see a lot of that in my portfolio. My work is greatly inspired by photographers of the past and present, most notably Henri Cartier-Bresson and Ethan Tran (ethan.uncurated).

My existence is captured in these pictures. I could do an entire thesis on what this passion means to me, but I’ll save you the pain for now.

I also really enjoy making pretty little videos, so please check that out too if you got the time! You can find all of my latest works on Instagram and YouTube.

THANK YOU SO MUCH for taking the time to check out my work and website. I hope to see you soon :)
